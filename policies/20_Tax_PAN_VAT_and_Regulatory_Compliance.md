**Location:** Kathmandu, Nepal
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-07-16
**Owner:** Finance & Legal

**Purpose**

**Registration and Documentation**
Maintain required registrations and licenses. Collect and validate necessary customer and seller information for invoicing and tax purposes. Issue compliant invoices and receipts, and retain records per retention schedules. Systems must be configured to calculate taxes accurately and present clear breakdowns at checkout.

**Filing and Remittance**
Prepare periodic filings according to regulatory cadence. Reconcile sales, refunds, and adjustments. Coordinate with auditors when applicable. Monitor regulatory changes and update systems and processes promptly.

**Controls and Assurance**
Segregate duties for tax calculation, approval, and payment. Access to financial systems follows least privilege and is audited. Exceptions and corrections are documented and approved. Training is provided to staff responsible for compliance.

**Communication and Review**
Communicate changes affecting customers (e.g., tax rate adjustments) transparently. Review this policy annually or upon regulatory change, and ensure alignment with broader compliance and privacy obligations.
