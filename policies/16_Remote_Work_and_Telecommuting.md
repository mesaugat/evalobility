# Remote Work & Telecommuting Policy

**Company:** wwktm
**Location:** Kathmandu, Nepal
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-10
**Owner:** HR & IT

**Purpose**
Define expectations and security requirements for employees working remotely, ensuring productivity, compliance, and protection of company data and customer privacy.

**Eligibility and Expectations**
Remote work arrangements require manager approval and clear deliverables. Employees must maintain availability during agreed working hours, use approved collaboration tools, and ensure a suitable work environment with proper ergonomics.

**Data and Communication**
Use company email and messaging for business communications. Record meeting notes and decisions in approved systems. Follow data classification rules when sharing information. Report any suspected security incidents immediately via designated channels.

**Monitoring and Support**
We may monitor access logs and performance metrics for compliance. IT provides remote support for approved software and devices. Changes to remote work terms will be communicated in advance.
