# Endpoint, BYOD & Mobile Device Policy

**Company:** wwktm
**Location:** Kathmandu, Nepal
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-12-03
**Owner:** IT & Security

**Purpose**
Protect company data accessed via endpoints (laptops, desktops) and mobile devices, including employee‑owned devices (BYOD), while maintaining usability for our e‑commerce operations.

**Requirements for Company Devices**
Device encryption, screen locks, regular OS updates, endpoint protection/EDR agents, and prohibited installation of unauthorized software. Administrative privileges are restricted. Logging and remote wipe capabilities must be enabled.

**BYOD Conditions**
Access to company data requires mobile device management (MDM) or containerization, minimum OS versions, security patches, and consent for remote wipe of company data. Personal data is not monitored; only company apps and containers are managed.

**Data Handling and Support**
Store company data in approved applications. Use VPN and MFA for remote access. Report lost or stolen devices immediately. IT provides support for approved configurations; unsupported devices may lose access.

**Compliance**
Periodic checks verify adherence. Violations may result in access revocation and disciplinary actions.
